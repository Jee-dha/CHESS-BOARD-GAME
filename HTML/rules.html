1. Objective of the Game

The aim is to checkmate your opponent's king. Checkmate occurs when the king is under attack (in check) and there is no legal move to escape the threat.

6. Touch-Move Rule

If a player touches a piece, they must move it if it's legal to do so. This applies in official tournaments.

7. Time Controls

Games may have time limits. Running out of time means losing the game unless the opponent cannot possibly checkmate.
